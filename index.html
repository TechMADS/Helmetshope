Our Story

BORN ON
THE ROAD

Fun Loaded started as a rider's dream in Pondicherry back in 2018. What began as a small corner shop on Mission Street has grown into the city's most trusted motorcycle gear destination.

We know the thrill of the ECR at dawn, the freedom of riding through French Quarter lanes, the joy of gearing up for a long weekend tour. Our shop is built from that passion.

Every helmet, jacket, glove and fitment we stock is hand-picked by our team of passionate riders. If it doesn't meet our standards, it doesn't reach our shelves.
